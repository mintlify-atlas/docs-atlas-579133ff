The tables below reproduce the quantitative results from the HY-World 2.0 technical report. WorldMirror 2.0 is evaluated on point map reconstruction across three standard datasets — 7-Scenes, NRGBD, and DTU — under multiple resolution and prior configurations. WorldStereo 2.0 is evaluated on camera control accuracy and the quality of single-view-generated reconstructions against competitive baselines.

## Effect of prior injection

Injecting geometric priors as conditioning inputs consistently improves WorldMirror 2.0 accuracy across all benchmarks. The three supported prior types — camera extrinsics, camera intrinsics, and per-view depth maps — can be used independently or together.

The "+all priors" rows in the reconstruction table above represent the strongest configuration: all three prior types supplied simultaneously. At high resolution (H) with all priors, WorldMirror 2.0 achieves its best results on 7-Scenes (**0.012 Acc., 0.016 Comp.**) and DTU (**0.554 Acc., 0.771 Comp.**).

See the [prior injection guide](/worldmirror/prior-injection) for instructions on preparing and passing camera and depth priors to the pipeline.
